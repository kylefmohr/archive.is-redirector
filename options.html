Domain Management

Add domains individually, or load a recommended set.

Format: Enter domains without "www." or "http://". For example, use "example.com" not "www.example.com" or "https://example.com".

How it works: When you visit any URL from the domains listed above, the extension will automatically redirect you to archive.is/newest/[original_url].

Behavior Options

If checked, only subpages (e.g., example.com/article.html) will be redirected, not the main domain page.
